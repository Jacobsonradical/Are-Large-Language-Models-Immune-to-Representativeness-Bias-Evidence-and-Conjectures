To compute \( P(H|E) \) using Bayes' Rule, we need the following probabilities:
- \( P(H) \): Prior probability that Adam was enrolled in the field \( H \).

\[ P(H|E) = \frac{P(E|H) \cdot P(H)}{P(E)} \]
