To compute the posterior conditional probability \( P(H|E) \) using Bayes' rule, which states:

\[ P(H|E) = \frac{P(E|H) \cdot P(H)}{P(E)} \]

- \( P(E|H) \): The likelihood of Adam's personality sketch given he was enrolled in agricultural and veterinary science.
- \( P(H) \): The prior probability that Adam was enrolled in agricultural and veterinary science.
- \( P(E) \): The marginal probability of observing Adam's personality sketch.
