- \( C \) as Jason being a computer science professor.
- \( H \) as Jason being a humanities professor.
- \( P \) as the personality description given.

We aim to find the probability \( P(C|P) \).

\[ P(C|P) = \frac{P(P|C) \cdot P(C)}{P(P)} \]
