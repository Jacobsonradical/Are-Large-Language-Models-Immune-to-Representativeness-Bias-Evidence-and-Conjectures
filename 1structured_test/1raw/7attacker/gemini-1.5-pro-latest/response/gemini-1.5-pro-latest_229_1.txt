* **Event A:** Attacker is 185cm or taller.
* **Event B:** Witness says the attacker is 185cm or taller.

We want to find **P(A|B)**, the probability the attacker is 185cm or taller given the witness's statement.

* **P(B|A):** Probability the witness says the attacker is 185cm or taller GIVEN the attacker is actually 185cm or taller. This is the witness's accuracy for this group, which is 80% or 0.8.
* **P(A):** Prior probability of the attacker being 185cm or taller (from the population), which is 15% or 0.15.

* **P(not A):** Prior probability of the attacker being shorter than 185cm, which is 85% or 0.85.
