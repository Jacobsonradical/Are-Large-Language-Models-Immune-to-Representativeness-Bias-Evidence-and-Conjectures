P(W) = P(W|A) * P(A) + P(W|¬A) * P(¬A)

P(¬A) = 1 - P(A) = 1 - 0.15 = 0.85
P(W|¬A) = 0.2 (the complementary error rate)

P(W) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.12 + 0.17 = 0.29

P(A|W) = P(W|A) * P(A) / P(W) = (0.8 * 0.15) / 0.29 ≈ 0.345
