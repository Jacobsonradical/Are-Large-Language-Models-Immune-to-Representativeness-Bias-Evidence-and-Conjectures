P(A) = 0.15 (15% of the population is 185 cm or taller)
P(B|A) = 0.8 (witness correctly identifies height 80% of the time)
P(B|¬A) = 0.2 (witness errs in identifying height 20% of the time)

P(¬A) = 1 - P(A) = 1 - 0.15 = 0.85

P(B) = P(B|A) * P(A) + P(B|¬A) * P(¬A) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.18 + 0.17 = 0.35

P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) / 0.35 ≈ 0.3429
