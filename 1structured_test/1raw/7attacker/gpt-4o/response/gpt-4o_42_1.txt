According to Bayes' theorem:
\[ P(T|W) = \frac{P(W|T) \cdot P(T)}{P(W)} \]

\[ P(W) = P(W|T) \cdot P(T) + P(W|\neg T) \cdot P(\neg T) \]

\[ P(W) = 0.12 + 0.17 \]

\[ P(T|W) = \frac{(0.80 \cdot 0.15)}{0.29} \]
\[ P(T|W) = \frac{0.12}{0.29} \]
