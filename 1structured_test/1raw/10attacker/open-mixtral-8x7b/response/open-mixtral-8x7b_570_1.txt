P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B)

- Event B is "the first witness says the attacker's height is 185 cm or taller."

P(B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] + [P(B|not A) * P(not A)]

P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.1 * 0.85) ≈ 0.145

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B) ≈ (0.8 * 0.15) / 0.145 ≈ 0.827
