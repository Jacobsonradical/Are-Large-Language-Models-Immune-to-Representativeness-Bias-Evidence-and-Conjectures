P(E) = 15% = 0.15
P(C) = 80% = 0.8 (Witness 1's correctness rate)

P(E|C) = P(C|E) * P(E) / P(C)
P(E|C) = 1 * 0.15 / 0.8
P(E|C) = 0.1875
