P(Attacker ≥ 185 cm | Witness 1) = P(Witness 1 true positive) * P(Attacker ≥ 185 cm) + P(Witness 1 false positive) * P(Attacker < 185 cm)

P(Witness 1 true positive and Attacker ≥ 185 cm) = P(Witness 1 true positive) * P(Attacker ≥ 185 cm)
