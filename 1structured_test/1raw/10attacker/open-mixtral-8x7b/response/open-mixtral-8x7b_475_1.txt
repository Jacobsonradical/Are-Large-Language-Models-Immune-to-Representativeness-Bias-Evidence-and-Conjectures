3. However, the witness statements are not independent, as they both observed the same person.

- A as the event that the attacker's height is 185 cm or taller
- W1 as the event that the first witness correctly identifies the height
- W2 as the event that the second witness correctly identifies the height

P(A|W1) = P(W1|A) * P(A) / P(W1)
