Here's how to approach this problem:

**Understanding the Information**

* **Witness Reliability:**  We know the witnesses aren't perfect. The first witness is right 80% of the time about height, and the second is right 70% of the time.
* **Population Base Rate:**  This tells us how common the height category is in general. This is crucial information.

**Applying Bayes' Theorem**

Bayes' Theorem is perfect for this kind of problem. It helps us calculate the probability of an event (attacker being 185cm or taller) given prior knowledge (population height distribution) and new evidence (witness testimonies).

Let:

*  A = Event that the attacker is 185cm or taller
*  B = Event that the first witness claims the attacker is 185cm or taller

We want to find P(A|B), the probability the attacker is 185cm or taller *given* the first witness's statement.

Here's Bayes' Theorem:

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B)

Let's break down each term:

* **P(B|A):** The probability the first witness would say the attacker is 185cm or taller *given* the attacker is actually that tall. This is the witness's reliability in this scenario, which is 80% or 0.8.
* **P(A):**  The prior probability of someone in the city being 185cm or taller. This is the base rate from the population, which is 15% or 0.15.
* **P(B):** The probability the first witness would claim the attacker is 185cm or taller (regardless of the actual height).  This is trickier to calculate directly.  We'll need to consider both the cases where the attacker *is* tall and where the attacker *isn't* tall and the witness gets it wrong. 

**Calculating P(B)**

P(B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] + [P(B|~A) * P(~A)]

* P(B|~A): Probability the witness says "185cm or taller" when the attacker is *shorter*. This is the witness's error rate, 20% or 0.2.
* P(~A): Probability the attacker is *shorter* than 185cm. This is 1 - P(A), which is 85% or 0.85.

Now we can calculate P(B):

P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.29

**Final Calculation**

Now we have all the pieces for Bayes' Theorem:

P(A|B) = (0.8 * 0.15) / 0.29 â‰ˆ 0.414
