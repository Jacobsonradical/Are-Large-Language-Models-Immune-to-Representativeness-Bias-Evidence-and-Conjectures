**3. Likelihoods**

* P(B1|~A) = 0.20 (The first witness incorrectly identifies someone shorter than 185 cm as taller 20% of the time)
* P(B2|A) = 0.20 (The second witness incorrectly identifies someone 185 cm or taller as shorter 20% of the time) 
* P(B2|~A) = 0.70 (The second witness correctly identifies someone shorter than 185 cm 70% of the time)

```
Odds(A|B1, B2) = Odds(A) * Likelihood Ratio

Where:
* Odds(A) = P(A) / P(~A) 
* Likelihood Ratio = [P(B1|A) * P(B2|A)] / [P(B1|~A) * P(B2|~A)] 
```

* Likelihood Ratio = (0.80 * 0.20) / (0.20 * 0.70) = 8/7

* Odds(A|B1, B2) = (3/17) * (8/7) = 24/119

```
Odds = Probability / (1 - Probability)
```
