**4. Apply Bayes' Theorem (We want P(T|W1), the probability the attacker is tall given the first witness's claim):**

 P(T|W1) = [P(W1|T) * P(T)] / P(W1) 

 To find P(W1), the probability of the first witness claiming the attacker is tall, we use the law of total probability:

 P(W1) = [P(W1|T) * P(T)] + [P(W1|S) * P(S)]
 P(W1) = (0.80 * 0.15) + (0.20 * 0.85) = 0.29

P(T|W1) = (0.80 * 0.15) / 0.29 = 0.414
