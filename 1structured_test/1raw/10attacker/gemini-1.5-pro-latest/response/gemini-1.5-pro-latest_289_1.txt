* **P(not A):** Prior probability of a person in the city being shorter than 185 cm = 0.85 (85%)

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B)

P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.29
