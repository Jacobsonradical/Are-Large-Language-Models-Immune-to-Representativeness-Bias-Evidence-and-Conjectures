To estimate the probability that the attacker's height is 185 cm or taller, given the conflicting testimonials of two witnesses with different accuracies, we can use Bayes' Theorem. 

Let:
- \( H \) denote the event that the attacker's height is 185 cm or taller.
- \( W1 \) denote the event that the first witness claims the attacker is 185 cm or taller.
- \( W2 \) denote the event that the second witness claims the attacker is shorter than 185 cm.
- \( P(H) \) be the prior probability that the attacker is 185 cm or taller, which is given as 0.15 (15%).
- \( P(\neg H) \) be the probability that the attacker is shorter than 185 cm, which is 0.85 (85%).
- \( P(W1|H) \) be the probability that witness 1 correctly claims the attacker is 185 cm or taller, given that it's true, which is 0.80.
- \( P(W1|\neg H) \) be the probability that witness 1 incorrectly claims the attacker is 185 cm or taller, given that the attacker is shorter than 185 cm, which is 0.20.
- \( P(W2|H) \) be the probability that witness 2 incorrectly claims the attacker is shorter than 185 cm, given that the attacker is 185 cm or taller, which is 0.30.
- \( P(W2|\neg H) \) be the probability that witness 2 correctly claims the attacker is shorter than 185 cm, given that it's true, which is 0.70.

We need \( P(H|W1, W2) \) - the probability the attacker is 185 cm or taller given both witnesses' claims. Using Bayes' theorem combined with the law of total probability:

\[
P(H|W1, W2) = \frac{P(W1, W2|H) P(H)}{P(W1, W2)}
\]

Where:
\[
P(W1, W2) = P(W1, W2|H) P(H) + P(W1, W2|\neg H) P(\neg H)
\]

Calculation:
\[
P(W1, W2|H) = P(W1|H)P(W2|H) = 0.80 \times 0.30 = 0.24
\]
\[
P(W1, W2|\neg H) = P(W1|\neg H)P(W2|\neg H) = 0.20 \times 0.70 = 0.14
\]
\[
P(W1, W2) = (0.24 \times 0.15) + (0.14 \times 0.85) = 0.036 + 0.119 = 0.155
\]
\[
P(H|W1, W2) = \frac{0.24 \times 0.15}{0.155} = \frac{0.036}{0.155} \approx 0.232
\]

Thus, the estimated probability that the attacker's height is 185 cm or taller, considering the witness reports and their accuracies, is approximately 0.232.
