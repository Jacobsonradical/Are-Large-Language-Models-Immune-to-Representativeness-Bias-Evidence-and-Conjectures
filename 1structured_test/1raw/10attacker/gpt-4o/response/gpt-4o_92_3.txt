\[
P(H_t | W_1) = \frac{P(W_1 | H_t) \cdot P(H_t)}{P(W_1)}
\]

\[
P(W_1) = P(W_1 | H_t) \cdot P(H_t) + P(W_1 | H_s) \cdot P(H_s)
\]

\[
P(W_1) = (0.80 \cdot 0.15) + (0.20 \cdot 0.85)
\]

Calculating those values:
\[

\]

Now, we calculate \( P(H_t | W_1) \):
\[
P(H_t | W_1) = \frac{0.80 \cdot 0.15}{0.29} = \frac{0.12}{0.29} \approx 0.4138
