\[ P(H_1 | E_1 \cap E_2) = \frac{P(E_1 \cap E_2 | H_1) \cdot P(H_1)}{P(E_1 \cap E_2)} \]

\[ P(H_1 | E_1 \cap E_2) = \frac{0.24 \cdot 0.15}{0.155} = \frac{0.036}{0.155} \approx 0.232 \]
