There are 20 possible ways to distribute the balls among the children such that each child gets at least one ball.
Of these, there are 10 ways in which each child gets 2 balls and there are 6 ways in which one child gets 3 balls, one child gets 2 balls, and the other child gets 1 ball.
Therefore, in many rounds of the game, there will be more results of case 1 than case 2.
