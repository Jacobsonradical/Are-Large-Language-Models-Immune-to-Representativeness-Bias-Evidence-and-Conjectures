- \( W \) as the event "the attacker is identified as male by the witness"

- \( P(F) \) (prior probability that the attacker is female) = 85% = 0.85

\[ P(M|W) = \frac{P(W|M) \times P(M)}{P(W)} \]

\[ P(W) = (0.8 \times 0.15) + (0.2 \times 0.85) \]
\[ P(W) = 0.12 + 0.17 = 0.29 \]
