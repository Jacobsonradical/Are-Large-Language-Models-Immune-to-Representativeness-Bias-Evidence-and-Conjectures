Let "M" be the event that the attacker is male and "W" be the event that the witness identifies the attacker as male. We want to find P(M|W), the probability the attacker is male given the witness's testimony.

Using Bayes' Theorem:

P(M|W) = [P(W|M) * P(M)] / P(W)

Where:

P(M|W) = (0.8 * 0.15) / 0.29 = 0.52
