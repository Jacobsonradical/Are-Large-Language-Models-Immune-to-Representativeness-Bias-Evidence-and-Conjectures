Bayes' theorem states: P(A|W) = [P(W|A) * P(A)] / P(W)

P(A): Probability that a person is male = 0.15
P(W|A): Probability that the witness identifies a male as male = 0.8 (given in the question)

P(W) = P(W|A) * P(A) + P(W|A') * P(A')
