- W: the event that the witness identified the attacker as male

- P(W|A): the probability of the witness identifying the attacker as male given that the attacker is male, which is 1 (since the attacker is definitely male)

- P(¬A): the prior probability that the attacker is not male, which is 0.85 (85% of the population)

P(A|W) = [P(W|A) \* P(A)] / [P(W|A) \* P(A) + P(W|¬A) \* P(¬A)]

P(A|W) = [1 \* 0.15] / [1 \* 0.15 + 0.2 \* 0.85]
