- B: the witness identified the attacker as male

- P(A) = 0.15 (the population is 15% male)
- P(B|not A) = 0.2 (the witness incorrectly identifies the attacker as male 20% of the time)
- P(not A) = 0.85 (the population is 85% female)

P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / (P(B|A) * P(A) + P(B|not A) * P(not A))

P(A|B) = (0.8 * 0.15) / (0.8 * 0.15 + 0.2 * 0.85)
