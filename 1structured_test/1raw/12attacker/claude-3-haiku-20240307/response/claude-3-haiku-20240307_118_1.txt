- B: The witness identified the attacker as male

Using Bayes' theorem:
P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B)

Given information:

- P(A) = 0.15 (the proportion of males in the population)
- P(B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) + (P(B|not A) * P(not A))

P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.44
