\[ P(M|W) = \frac{P(W|M) \cdot P(M)}{P(W)} \]

\[ P(W) = P(W|M) \cdot P(M) + P(W|F) \cdot P(F) \]

- \( P(M) \) is the prior probability that the attacker is male.
- \( P(F) \) is the prior probability that the attacker is female.
- \( P(W|M) \) is the probability that the witness identifies the attacker as male given that the attacker is actually male.
