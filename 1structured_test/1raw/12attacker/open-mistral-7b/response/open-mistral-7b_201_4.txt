- P(E|H) is the probability of the evidence given the hypothesis (witness's accuracy rate when identifying males)
- P(H) is the prior probability of the hypothesis (probability of the attacker being male in the population)
- P(E) is the probability of the evidence (regardless of the hypothesis)
