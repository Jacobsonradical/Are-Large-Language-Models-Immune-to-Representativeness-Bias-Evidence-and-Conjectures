- A: The event that the attacker is male
- ¬A: The event that the attacker is not male (i.e., the attacker is female)

P(A|E) = [P(E|A) * P(A)] / [P(E|A) * P(A) + P(E|¬A) * P(¬A)]

P(A|E) = [0.8 * 0.15] / [0.8 * 0.15 + 0.2 * 0.85]
