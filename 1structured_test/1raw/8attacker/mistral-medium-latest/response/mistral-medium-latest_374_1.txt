Bayes' theorem states:

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) \* P(A)] / P(B)

P(B|A) = 0.8 (the witness correctly identifies a tall person as tall 80% of the time)

P(B) = P(B and A) + P(B and not A)

P(A|B) = (0.8 \* 0.15) / 0.285 ≈ 0.421
