P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B)

P(B) = P(B|A) * P(A) + P(B|A') * P(A')

P(A') = 1 - P(A) = 1 - x

P(B) = P(B|A) * P(A) + P(B|A') * P(A')
= (0.8 * x) + (x * (1 - x))

= (0.8 * x) / ((0.8 * x) + (x * (1 - x)))
