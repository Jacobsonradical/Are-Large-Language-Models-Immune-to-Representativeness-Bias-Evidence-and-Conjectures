**Calculating the Probability**

* **Event A:** The attacker is 185 cm or taller.
* **Event B:** The witness identifies the attacker as 185 cm or taller.

We want to find **P(A|B):** the probability the attacker is 185 cm or taller *given* the witness said they were.

Here's the formula:

```
P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B) 
```
