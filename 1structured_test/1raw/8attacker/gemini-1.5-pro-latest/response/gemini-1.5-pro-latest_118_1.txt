Here's how to break down the problem and calculate the probability:

**Understanding the Information**

* **Prior Probability (from information "a"):** This tells us the general likelihood of an attacker being a certain height within the entire population of potential attackers. 
    * P(Under 185 cm) = 0.85
    * P(185 cm or Taller) = 0.15 
* **Witness Reliability (from information "b"):** This tells us how much we can trust the witness's testimony.
    * P(Correctly Identifying Height) = 0.80
    * P(Incorrectly Identifying Height) = 0.20

**Applying Bayes' Theorem**

Bayes' Theorem is perfect for this situation. It lets us update our prior belief (the general attacker height distribution) with new evidence (the witness testimony).

Here's the formula:

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B)

Where:

* **P(A|B):** The probability of event A happening given that event B happened.  In our case: The probability the attacker is 185cm or taller *given* the witness said so. This is what we want to find.
* **P(B|A):** The probability of event B happening given that event A happened. In our case: The probability the witness would say the attacker is 185cm or taller *given* the attacker actually is 185cm or taller (This is the witness's reliability in this specific scenario - 80% or 0.8).
* **P(A):** The prior probability of event A. In our case: The prior probability an attacker is 185cm or taller (0.15).
* **P(B):** The probability of event B happening (regardless of whether A happened or not). We can calculate this using the law of total probability: 
    * P(B) = P(B|A) * P(A)  +  P(B|~A) * P(~A) 
    * Where ~A is the event of the attacker being *under* 185cm.

**Calculations**

1. **P(B):**
   * P(B|A) = 0.8 (witness correct when attacker is tall)
   * P(A) = 0.15 (prior probability of tall attacker)
   * P(B|~A) = 0.2 (witness incorrect when attacker is short)
   * P(~A) = 0.85 (prior probability of short attacker)
   * P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.29 

2. **P(A|B):** (Putting it all together)
   * P(A|B) = (0.8 * 0.15) / 0.29
