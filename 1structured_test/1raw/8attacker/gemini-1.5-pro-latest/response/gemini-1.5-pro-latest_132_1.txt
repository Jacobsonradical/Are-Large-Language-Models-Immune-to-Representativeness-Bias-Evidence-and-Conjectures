* **P(not A) = 0.85** (Prior probability of an attacker being shorter than 185 cm)

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / P(B)

P(B) =  [P(B|A) * P(A)] + [P(B| not A) * P(not A)]
P(B) = (0.8 * 0.15) + (0.2 * 0.85) = 0.29
