* **P(Attacker tall | Witness says tall) = [P(Witness says tall | Attacker tall) * P(Attacker tall)] / P(Witness says tall) **

* **P(Witness says tall | Attacker tall):** The probability the witness says the attacker is tall, given they are actually tall (80% or 0.8).
* **P(Attacker tall):** The prior probability the attacker is tall (15% or 0.15).
* **P(Witness says tall):** The probability the witness says the attacker is tall (we'll calculate this).

Therefore, P(Witness says tall) = 0.12 + 0.17 = 0.29
